Darshan Raval was born on October 18, 1994 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. He is a music artist and actor, known for Shershaah (2021), Ludo (2020) and Rocky Aur Rani Kii Prem Kahaani (2023). He has been married to Dharal Surelia since January 18, 2025. According to me , He is the best singer !!
